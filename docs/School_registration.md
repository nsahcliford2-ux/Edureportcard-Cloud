# School Registration

## Registration Steps

1. School creates an account.
2. Email verification.
3. Phone verification (future).
4. Choose a subscription plan.
5. Complete payment.
6. School account activated.
7. Unique School ID generated.
8. Dashboard created automatically.

## Subscription

- Starter
- Standard
- Premium

## Subscription Duration

- 1 Year

## Renewal

- Email reminders
- Dashboard reminders
- Online renewal
